The Social Media Registry API can be paired with the APIs of social-media services to reveal amazing work being done across government. See the demonstrations below for examples of what's possible.

Flickr

Many sites listed in the Registry are on Flickr, a photo sharing service. U.S. Government accounts post photos from the latest news to the oldest archives, in many areas of focus. This demo highlights recent photos from across government and makes them easy to share.

Demonstrate Flickr photos »

Twitter

Social media accounts on Twitter allow agencies and organizations to interact directly with constituents. The USA.gov Twitter account aggregates lists of official U.S. Government Twitter accounts organized by agency; each list is kept current by regular automated updates from the Social Media Registry.
